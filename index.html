A Dashboard Report on the AirBnB Listings in New York City.
Dataset Obtained From Kaggle.

Market Overview Takeaways:

Private Spaces Are Most Common
Both private homes and rooms, make up a combined total of 98.72% of all listings. Out of the 33030 total listings, 19.2k are private homes, 13.3k are private rooms, 422 are shared rooms, and 112 are hotel rooms.


Brooklyn & Manhattan Centralized
Brooklyn and Manhattan alone make up 25,677 out of 33030 total listings. Breaking it down further, 16.19% and 23.07% of total listings are private rooms and homes respectively in Brooklyn; 12.94% and 24.35% of total listings are private rooms and homes respectively in Manhattan.


Manhattan Is More Expensive
The average price of the whole market is $182.07 per night with an average minimum number of nights of 16.35. However if we look at within only Manhattan specifically, the average price steeply hikes to $238.25 per night with an average minimum number of nights of 19.69. Even Manhattan's market share breatheren, Brooklyn, has a significantly lower average price of $160.20 with an average minimum number of nights of 15.81.
