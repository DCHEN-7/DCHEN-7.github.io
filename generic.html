Power BI Project

A Dashboard Report on the AirBnB Listings in New York City.
Dataset Obtained From Kaggle.

Summary & Insights:
Most common listings are private spaces within Manhattan or Brooklyn
Staten Island shared rooms followed by private lofts in Brooklyn are the most booked throughout the year
Most popular listings are under $100 USD a night and will have 12-14 reviews left per month
Most popular listings are close in proximity to the airport and explicitly state "JFK" in their title

Key Takeaway: If you're an looking to host an AirBnB in New York City, aim to list a private space under $100 per night near JFK airport while explicitly stating that close proximity in the title.
